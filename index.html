Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Inno Bubble Bloom is an offline mobile puzzle game developed by Inno Games. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting user information.

Information Collection

Inno Bubble Bloom does not collect, store, use, or share personal information from users. The game:

  • does not require account creation
  • does not include advertising
  • does not use analytics services
  • does not use third-party tracking technologies
  • does not collect location information
  • does not collect personal identifiers
  • can be played fully offline

Unity Engine

The game is built using the Unity game engine. Unity-related services such as Ads Mediation, Analytics, Diagnostics, and In-App Purchasing are disabled and not used within the application.

For more information about Unity’s privacy practices, please visit:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Children’s Privacy

Inno Bubble Bloom is not directed toward children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from users.

Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or monetization SDKs.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
